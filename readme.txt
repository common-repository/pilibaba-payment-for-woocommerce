== Description ==
This plugin helps you to enable Pilibaba Payment checkout for Chinese customers. 
Pilibaba Payment is a convenient tool of CNY payment, solves the complicated cumbersome process of customs inspection and the problem of goods detain, and provides one-stop transport logistics services to saving shipping cost and time for the shippers.

[youtube https://youtu.be/XvTfYR4RmfE]

Pilibaba is a platform that connects Global e-Commerce website with the Chinese consumers through Local Payment, International Shipment, Marketing and after-sale service. Pilibaba allows you to sell and deliver products directly to individual Chinese consumers through your existing e-commerce website.

China is the world's largest consuming market. The e-Shopping in China last year is USD2.76 trillion, outgrown USA's 1.80 trillion at 1.5 times. More important, Chinese overseas shoppers are fast growing as well. It is expected the market value will exceed 100 billion in 2015. What really matters and what Chinese consumers really care about are the User Experience / Payment Method / With/Without Direct Shipment / Aftersales Service etc... which are completely and easily handled by Pilibaba.

With Pilibaba support, your website is able to have the following functions for Chinese customers:

 * Chinese user friendly at shopping experience on your site
 * Accept Chinese bank debit cards and credit cards
 * International shipping to China with duty/tax and customs clearance service in Cheaper and faster
 * 7/24/365 after-sales service center in China
 * Brand awareness and drive traffic to your website

All you need to do is to integrate with our API which is only a few lines of codes. There will be no big changes to your existing website structure. We will do all the rest for you.

With regarding of the shipment, all you need to do is to ship the parcels to one of our designated warehouse in your homecountry. Pilibaba will do the rest things. For example: international shipment, customs clearance and last mile delivery.

The only fee we charge is 4.4% transactions fee which includes currency exchange fee. All the payment risk will be on Pilibaba. You will be able to see all the transactions and payment confirmation before shipment.

The source of Chinese consumers to your website will come from:

 * 1st, featured selected products in our hot-sale page which could reach 500-1000 orders per day.
 * 2nd, digital marketing campaigns to build your brand/product awareness and push sales, such as display, PPC, social, email. We are open to discussions for marketing plans and hoping that you could also launch special promotion for our members.
 * 3rd, joint-promotion campaigns with our partnering sites, such as customized email campaigns to premium bank account members.

We will also support your sales with Mandarin specialists in China for any questions from customers, including return, recall, etc. We will help collect return products if there will be and ship them back to you.

You could also visit our site for a demo of how consumers complete a transaction in Pilibaba: http://en.pilibaba.com/demo

For more information, please visit our website: www.pilibaba.com

Thanks for choosing Pilibaba!

== Installation ==

= Minimum Requirements =

* WooCommerce 2.1 or higher

= Automatic installation =

Automatic installation is the easiest option as WordPress handles the file transfers itself and you don't need to leave your web browser. To do an automatic install of Pilibaba Payment for WooCommerce, log in to your WordPress dashboard, navigate to the Plugins menu and click Add New.

In the search field type Pilibaba Payment for WooCommerce and click Search Plugins. Once you've found our plugin you can view details about it such as the the rating and description. Most importantly, of course, you can install it by simply clicking Install Now.

= Manual Installation =

1. Unzip the files and upload the folder into your plugins folder (/wp-content/plugins/) overwriting older versions if they exist
2. Activate the plugin in your WordPress admin area.
 
= Usage = 

1. Open the settings page for WooCommerce and click the "Checkout" tab
2. Click on the sub-item for Pilibaba Payment.
3. Enter your API credentials and adjust any other settings to suit your needs. 

= Updating = 

Automatic updates should work great for you.  As always, though, we recommend backing up your site prior to making any updates just to be sure nothing goes wrong.
